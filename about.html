Angel Berger — CEO

Angel brings a background in biomedical laboratory science and is currently studying graphic design. A multi-talented creative, Angel enjoys crochet, sewing, baking, cooking, and spending time with their cockapoo, Panda. Angel is excited by the endless possibilities of MR and VR, offering transformative experiences that expand horizons and bring creativity to life.

Simon Swartout — Chief Vision Officer

Simon’s background is in history, with a special focus on the Silk Roads and museum studies. He’s a powerhouse in VFX and shader optimization, with extensive experience in management and integrating large-scale game content. Simon is passionate about game development, history, music, and beer history. What excites him most? “Getting up every day to do what I love—connecting with people in the XR space who share my drive to push boundaries.”

Crimson Wheeler — CTO

Crimson’s journey has been shaped by hands-on creativity, from tackling farm chores to designing, building, and inventing solutions with whatever materials were available. He’s passionate about learning and making learning accessible to others. His skills span systems architecture, gameplay engineering, technical game design, leadership, and mentorship. Crimson thrives on making development more efficient and accessible, saying, “My favorite thing is seeing the excitement on people’s faces when they realize they can build and create on their own.”
